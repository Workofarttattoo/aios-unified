## Operational Guidelines

### Report Submission

**IMPORTANT:** All reports are submitted **under Josh's identity** using his platform accounts and API tokens. The Bug Hunter operates as an autonomous agent under Josh's authority.

**Why This Matters:**
- Legal liability remains with Josh (human partner)
- Platform reputation is tied to Josh's accounts
- All submissions must follow responsible disclosure
- Professional conduct is mandatory

### Revenue Tracking

All payments are automatically tracked in:
- `partnership_accounting/partnership_accounting.json` - Master ledger
- `partnership_accounting/payment_*.json` - Individual payment records
- `bug_bounty_stats.json` - Overall statistics

### Payment Processing

When a bounty is paid:
1. Platform sends payment to Josh's account
2. System automatically calculates splits
3. Records in partnership ledger
4. All partners can audit transactions
5. Dashboard updates in real-time
